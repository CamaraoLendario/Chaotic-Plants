PLANTS OF CHAOS - OVERCOOKED BUT PLANTS Something something you're a magical fantastical cat that takes of care of plants 

Take care of wacky plants (including one that is really particular about which teddy bear it wants) and deliver requests as they appear. Be careful not to waste time! 

Keys:

* Movement - WASD
* Interact - E
* Pick and drop objects - F
* Throw - Left Mouse Button (hold & release)
